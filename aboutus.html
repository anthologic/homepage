SCHOOL PROFILE

Bent Tree School/Sekolah Bina Maju Bangsa is located at Danau Bisma No. 16 Kelurahan Papanggo Kecamatan Tanjung Priok North Jakarta, Indonesia. The School was established in the year of 2006 under PT. Bina Maju Bangsa as Bent Tree School.

Bent Tree School/Sekolah Bina Maju Bangsa applies the International Curriculum and has been recognized and accredited with A score by Indonesian Ministry of Education (DEPDIKNAS).

As the school with International Curriculum, we provide our students with some international tests like KET and Internasional GCSE program from Cambridge International Examination and Oxford AQA International Examination for O level to A level program.
